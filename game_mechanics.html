About Fortnite 

What is fortnite? 

Fortnite is a highly popular online multiplayer free-to-play third-person battle royale shooter where 100 players are dropped onto an ever-shrinking island, fighting to be the last team or person standing. This game also includes a building mechanic which allows players to construct structures for cover and also offers a strategic advantage. This unique building system allows players to get materials from the surroundings to construct these structures. Fortnite has a diverse arsenal with a constantly evolving landscape and seasonal updates. Don’t forget about Fortnite's diverse heals and mobility constantly being added to the game to move faster around the map and keep your health up. Fortnite also includes cross-platform, social interactions online and in-game purchases.

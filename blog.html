Blog 1:

According to the article, people play games because of novelty, competence, exploration, risk-taking, safe place to fail. Novelty because games keep life fun and engaging because they allow you to do something new. Competence because many video games require skill, which if you get good at it makes you feel mastery or control over the videogame. Exploration because it can make you explore what you like doing or what you want or explore an open world of a game. Risk-taking because in a virtual world taking risks can’t hurt you in real life, but you can lose your match or your rank. A safe place to fail because if you die, or lose a game you can play another and be used as a skill in the real world because you learn from your mistakes.

 Toxicity in Gaming is when gamers rationalize by harassing others in a video game in different ways. This is important to understand because it can have negative long-term effects and can lead to depressive and suicidal thoughts in players getting harassed. To combat this issue big companies like EA, Infinity Ward, and Valve have developed a moderation tool to stop players from being toxic.
